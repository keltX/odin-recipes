Honey Garlic Chicken Recipe

Description

These wings are easy to make in the air fryer and will satisfy all your cravings for a tasty and indulgent snack. Whether you're hosting a party or just looking for a delicious appetizer, these wings are sure to impress your guests and have them begging for the recipe!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon of baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black peppper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 2 pounds of chiken wings
  • 1/2 cup of honey
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of grated fresh ginger
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons of green onions

Steps

  1. Preheat the airfryer to 200 degrees C
  2. Mix baking powder, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, paprika powder, and cayenne peper together in a small bowl
  3. Pat the chicken wings dry with paper towel, then toss them in spice mixture until evenly coated. Arrange wigns in a single layer in the airfryer basket
  4. Cook wings in the preheated air fryer until crispy and golden brown, juices are clear, and meat is no longer pink at the bone, 20 to 25 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 74 degrees C
  5. Meanwhile, make the honey garlic sauce. Stir honey, soy sauce, garlic, and ginger together in a small saucepan over medium heat.
  6. In a separate small bowl, whisk together cornstarch and water until smooth, then add to the saucepan. Cook until sauce thickens, 1 to 2 minutes
  7. Transfer wings into large bowl, pour honey garlic sauce over the wngs and toss to coat it evenly. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot.
