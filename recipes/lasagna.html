Lasagna's Recipes

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too.

Prepare this easy homemade lasagne ahead of time and save in the freezer, uncooked, for when you need it during a busy week. Then just bake for an extra 45 mins.

Ingredients

  • 2 Olive oil, plus a little for the dish
  • 750g lean beef mince
  • 90g pack of prosciutto
  • tomato sauce
  • 200ml hot beef stock
  • a little grated nutmeg
  • 300g pack fresh lasagne sheets
  • white sauce
  • 125g ball mozarella, torn into thin strips

Steps

  1. Making the meat sauce, heat 2 tbsp of olive oil in a frying pan and cook 750g lean beef mince in two batches for about 10 mins until browned all over.
  2. Finely chop 4 slices of prosciutto from a 90g pack, then stir through the meat mixture.
  3. Pour over 800g of uncooked tomato puree and 200ml hot beef stock. Add a little grated nutmeg and season
  4. Bring up the boil, then simmer for 30 mins until the sauce looks rich
  5. Heat oven to 180C and lightly oil an ovenproof dish
  6. Spoon one third of the meat sauce into the dish, then cover with some fresh lasagne sheets from a 300g pack. Drizzle over roughly 130g ready-mate or homemade white sauce
  7. Repeat until you have 3 layers of pasta. Cover with the remaining 390g white sauce, making sure you can't see any pasta poking through
  8. Scatter 125g torn mozzarela over the top
  9. Arrange the rest of the prosciutto on top. Bake for 45 mins until the top is bubbling and lightly browned
  10. Ready to serve
